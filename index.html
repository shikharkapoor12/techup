Welcome to GovConnect!


GovConnect is your gateway to a more interconnected and informed public sector.


GovConnect enables public officers to quickly identify and collaborate with colleagues who are currently or have previously have managed similar projects.


Key Features

  • Expertise Search: Utilize a powerful search engine to find public officers based on their current & past projects, and roles.

  • Task Publisher: Create and publish your own tasks or projects, enabling other public officers to discover your initiatives and connect with you for collaboration.

    What are the rules?

    To ensure a collaborative and reciprocal environment, our platform operates under a single guiding principle:

    Visibility is earned through contribution.

  • You will gain access to view others' tasks only when you share your own.

  • The extent of your access correlates directly with your level of activity; frequent contributions will unlock comprehensive visibility across all agency's tasks.

  • So engage actively and unlock the full potential of our database.
